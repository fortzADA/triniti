Mission

Faith-rooted. Nonprofit. Community-first.

1
Serve Romanian Orthodox parish life with clear church-event communication.
2
Operate as a nonprofit — not commercial social media.
3
Seek endorsement from the Consulate in Los Angeles and the Patriarchate.
4
Help Romanians help each other with practical work tasks and needs.
5
Uphold dignity, privacy, and Orthodox Christian values.

What Trinity offers

One platform for parish life and mutual support

Church & parish

  • Parish news feed & spiritual community posts
  • Groups for parishes, feast days & ministries
  • Event updates members can follow and share
  • Member profiles for recognition & trust
  • In-app notifications for community activity

Community & mutual help

  • Direct messages for private coordination
  • Help needed / help offered task posts
  • Groups for trades, volunteering & support
  • Native Android & iOS apps (Capacitor)
  • Secure email & social authentication
